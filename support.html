FAQ

Frequently asked

Do I need an Interactive Brokers account?

Yes. DealTitan executes against your own IBKR account through TWS or IB Gateway. We never take custody of your funds or place orders outside your authorization.

Is paper trading really free of risk?

Paper trades never place real orders. They track real IBKR bid/ask prices and apply the same stop logic as live trades, so you get a realistic preview without risking capital.

How quickly do config changes take effect?

Hot-reloadable settings — position size, stops, ML thresholds, and dry-run flags — apply within five minutes with no restart. Trades already open keep the settings they entered with.

Can I run some strategies live and others on paper?

Absolutely. Each scanner has its own dry-run flag, so you can keep proven strategies live while testing new ones on paper at the same time.

What happens to open positions at market close?

DealTitan automatically closes all positions at 3:55 PM EST. 0DTE options with no bid are recorded as expired worthless; everything else is sold at the current bid.
