Capabilities

Everything an execution desk needs

From signal to fill to exit — Acme Corp handles the whole lifecycle so you can focus on strategy, not order tickets.

ML signal filtering

Every signal is scored 0–100% by a continuously retrained model. Set a confidence floor and let weak setups pass you by.

Stair-step trailing stops

Profit milestones progressively tighten your stop and raise your target — locking gains while letting winners run to +400%.

Paper trading mode

Simulate fills on real IBKR bid/ask with realistic slippage. Prove a strategy for a day before flipping a single scanner live.

Seven strategy scanners

Wedge, SPY pivots, Bounce-and-Run, reversal and more — toggle each between live and paper independently.

Hot-reloadable config

Position size, stops, ML thresholds and dry-run flags update in under five minutes — no restart, no missed signals.

Runs on your hardware

A native desktop app that connects to your own IBKR account. Your keys, your machine — nothing custodial.

Getting started

Live in four steps

Most traders are paper-trading within the hour.

Connect IBKR

Run TWS or IB Gateway and enable the API. Acme Corp connects locally on port 4002.

Configure

Set your position size and pick which scanners run. Start every strategy in dry-run.

Paper trade

Watch simulated fills on real market data. Review outcomes and ML confidence.

Go live

Flip a scanner to live when you're ready. Changes apply within five minutes.

Our support assistant answers most setup questions instantly — IBKR connection, config, paper vs. live, billing. Try it now.
